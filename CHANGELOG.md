# [1.0.3] - 2017-04-17
### Added
- Functionality to assess entropy mining strength.

- ECC encrypts and decrypts buffers.
- Changed SHA-3 to FIPS 202 (F1600, XOF d=0x06).
- Dependency management during pre-install.
